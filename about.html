Ella Rossi is an 18 year old singer/songwriter based in Los Angeles, California. She comes from a rich cultural background with a mix of Italian, Colombian, Russian and Jewish origins. She started her singing career at the age of 10 when she was featured on Zecchino D’oro, an Italian children’s music show produced by RAI International. Her song, ‘Regalerò Un Sogno’ won several times and became one of the number one hits for children that year throughout Italy.

 

Ella was also featured in an Argentinian Broadway Production, “Wunderkindz” as one of the lead soloists. The production took place in 2014 in Buenos Aires and was sponsored by Warner Chappell Music Latin America. Ella was also recognized by ASCAP (American Society of Musicians and Composers) for her participation in that musical.

 

Some of her more recent endeavors have been some local performances in Los Angeles and San Diego. Ella was sought out by Mirrored Media Productions to be featured as the lead role in a modern interpretation of the NutCracker at the Americana at Brand Christmas Tree Lighting Ceremony. She was lucky enough to work alongside Ava Michelle (Tall Girl Netflix) and a local magician, Joseph Tran. In addition, she opened for singer/songwriter Renee Dominique who was featured on a song with Jason Mraz at a benefit concert for homeless teens, Song For Shelter.

 

Today, Ella sees herself as an R&B/Pop/Jazz vocalist. She has performed with a number of jazz bands around LA, including the Count Basie Quintet. She is now working to pursue her career as an R&B/Pop artist and hopes to share her music with the world.
